Welcome to my kitchen!

I'm Yuly, a Latina born in Colombia with a culinary journey that started in Toronto, where I attended culinary school and spent several years working behind the line. A few years ago, I moved to Miami and took my career in a new direction by following a hidden passion—marketing. While enjoying this exciting adventure, I realized I could blend my love for food with my newfound expertise, and that’s how @onehungrychica was born.

@onehungrychica aims to help people discover the best restaurants in Miami and share delicious, easy-to-make recipes for those who love to cook at home. Whether you’re exploring new flavors in your kitchen or looking for the next great spot to enjoy a meal, I’m here to guide, inspire, and celebrate with you.

In addition to sharing culinary content, I offer marketing, content creation, and social media management services for food brands and restaurants across Miami and South Florida. My goal is to help you build a strong digital presence and connect with your audience through engaging and authentic content.

If you’d like to connect, have a question about a recipe, or are interested in collaborating, don’t hesitate to reach out! You can email me at onehungrychica@gmail.com or follow me on social @onehungrychica and send me a DM.

Thank you for stopping by!

Xoxo,
Yuly
