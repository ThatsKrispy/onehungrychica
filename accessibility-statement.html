Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Our Commitment

One Hungry Chica Enterprise LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards to our website at www.onehungrychica.com.

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines define requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. Conformance to these guidelines helps make the web more accessible to users with visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, language, learning, and neurological disabilities.

Accessibility Features on This Site

We have implemented the following features to improve accessibility:

  • Keyboard navigation: All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard. Use Tab to navigate, Enter or Space to activate, and Escape to close dialogs.
  • Skip to content link: A "Skip to main content" link appears at the top of every page for screen reader and keyboard users.
  • Semantic HTML: We use proper HTML landmarks (header, main, nav, aside, footer) to help screen readers navigate page structure.
  • ARIA labels: All interactive elements, icons, and non-text content carry ARIA labels or descriptions.
  • Image alt text: Every image includes descriptive alternative text that communicates its purpose.
  • Color contrast: Text and background colors meet or exceed WCAG AA contrast ratios (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text).
  • Focus indicators: Visible focus outlines are present on all focusable elements.
  • Responsive design: The site is fully responsive and works at all zoom levels up to 400% without loss of content.
  • Accessible forms: All form fields have associated labels, required fields are marked, and error messages are descriptive.
  • No auto-playing media: No audio or video content plays automatically.

Accessibility Toolbar

We provide a built-in accessibility toolbar (the blue circle icon in the bottom-left corner of every page) with the following options:

  • Adjustable font size (70%–160%)
  • High contrast mode
  • Dark mode
  • Color inversion
  • Dyslexia-friendly readable font
  • Underline all links
  • Enlarged cursor
  • Highlighted focus indicators
  • Pause all animations
  • Reading guide line
  • Color blindness filters (Protanopia, Deuteranopia, Tritanopia)

Your accessibility preferences are saved in your browser so they persist across visits.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full WCAG 2.1 AA conformance, some third-party embedded content (such as social media widgets or external links) may not meet all accessibility standards. We are working to improve or replace these components over time.

Technical Specifications

This website is built with semantic HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript. It has been tested with the following assistive technologies:

  • NVDA screen reader (Windows)
  • VoiceOver (macOS and iOS)
  • TalkBack (Android)
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Windows High Contrast Mode

Feedback & Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of One Hungry Chica. If you experience barriers accessing any content or functionality, please contact us:

  • Email: onehungrychica@gmail.com
  • Subject line: Accessibility Feedback

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 2 business days and to provide an accessible alternative or resolve the issue within 10 business days.
