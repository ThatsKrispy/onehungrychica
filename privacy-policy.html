1. Who We Are

One Hungry Chica Enterprise LLC ("One Hungry Chica", "we", "our", "us") operates www.onehungrychica.com, a Miami-based food blog and digital marketing agency founded by Yuly Rodriguez. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Data Controller: One Hungry Chica Enterprise LLC, Miami, FL, USA
Contact: onehungrychica@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email address, and message content.
  • Newsletter sign-ups: email address.

Information collected automatically (with consent):

  • Usage data via Google Analytics: pages visited, time on site, referring URLs, browser type, device type, and approximate geographic location (country/region level).
  • Cookies — see Section 4 below.

Information we do NOT collect: We do not collect payment information, government IDs, passwords, or sensitive personal data as defined under GDPR. We do not sell your personal information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your messages and inquiries.
  • To send newsletters you explicitly opted into (you may unsubscribe at any time).
  • To analyze website usage and improve content — only after you consent to analytics cookies.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.
  • To protect the security and integrity of our website.

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to operate our site and, with your consent, to analyze traffic. We use a cookie consent banner that appears on your first visit. You can manage your preferences at any time via the banner or by using the "Manage Preferences" option.

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functions (e.g., remembering your cookie preferences and accessibility settings). These are always active and cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): Set only if you accept analytics cookies. These help us understand how visitors use the site. The data is anonymized and aggregated.

To opt out of Google Analytics across all sites, you may use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent — for analytics cookies and newsletter subscriptions. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests — for responding to contact form inquiries, where our interest in communicating with you is not overridden by your privacy rights.
  • Legal obligation — where required by applicable law.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only with:

  • Google Analytics (analytics processor) — anonymized traffic data, only with your consent. Google may process this data on servers outside your country.
  • Legal authorities — if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of others.

We do not use advertising networks, retargeting pixels, or data brokers.

7. Data Retention

  • Contact form messages: retained for up to 12 months, then deleted.
  • Newsletter emails: retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Analytics data: retained per Google's standard retention period (14 months by default).
  • Cookie consent records: retained for 12 months.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Deletion ("right to be forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit processing of your data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw analytics or newsletter consent at any time.

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal data is collected, to delete it, to opt out of its sale (we do not sell data), and to non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at onehungrychica@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

Our website is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption). We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect the information we collect. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Links to Other Sites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (social media platforms, brands, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
